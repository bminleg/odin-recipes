Pasta recipe

Description

Pasta is a type of food made from a mixture of flour, eggs, and water that is formed into different shapes and then boiled. Spaghetti, macaroni, and noodles are types of pasta.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 1/2 cups flour (“00” flour, semolina flour, all-purpose flour — or a blend)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt.

Steps

  1. Boil Water. Start with a very large pot of water, about 6 quarts per pound of pasta.
  2. Add Salt.
  3. Add the Pasta.
  4. Stir.
  5. Taste the Pasta.
  6. Drain
  7. Removing Ravioli.
  8. Stir in the Sauce.
